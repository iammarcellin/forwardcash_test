How to send Money

First, create an account by signing up with us. Thereafter, login to your account, select to which country you are sending money to and select the payment option for which your recipient will be receiving the money by and then enter the amount which your recipient will be receiving in local currency so that you can be informed how much money you need to send in your own currency. After, complete the recipient details as it will be shown on the form and finally make the payment using the payment options available on our platform.

How to receive money

The receiver does not need to have an account with us, the payment sent will be paid to their mobile money account numbers provided by the senders
